    About Me

    I am an Information Systems Graduate from Stony Brook University who took a slightly different path and ventured into the world of teahing. While teaching has been a rewarding experience, I have always held a deep-rooted passion for coding and software development. It was during a moment of helping a student with their introductory computer science class that I was reminded of this passion. I love being able to bring my ideas to life through code and I enjoy tackling the complex challenges that come with it. As I gradually transitioned away from tutoring, I devoted myself to exploring and learning through online resources like The Odin Project. Nowadays, I spend majority of my time learning and coding but when I'm not, I enjoy weightlifting, playing handball, and cooking. These activities help me recharge and refocus, allowing me to take on more challenges in my work. Technology advances quickly, and I am eager to continue learning and take on new challenges in creating innovative software applications. Feel free to check out my projects, and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or simply want to chat.
